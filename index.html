About

Welcome to the Radiation Detection Laboratory led by Trinayan Saikia, PhD Research Scholar in Nuclear Physics. The research focuses on development of compact neutron and gamma-ray detectors using SiPM technology and liquid scintillators.

Research Areas

  • Radiation Detection Instrumentation
  • SiPM Characterization
  • Organic Scintillator Gamma Spectroscopy
  • Geant4 Detector Simulation
  • Gamma Spectrum Unfolding Algorithms

Current Work

Development of portable neutron/gamma detector system integrating SiPM arrays, Arduino-based DAQ and Monte Carlo simulation validation.
